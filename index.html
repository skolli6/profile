About Me

15 Years Building What Moves the World

"I architect embedded systems that run reliably at the edge of the physical world — from plant-floor PLCs to satellite-connected vehicles to smart cities."

I'm Srihari Kolli, an Embedded Systems & IoT Architect with 17+ years of experience delivering mission-critical software for automotive, industrial, and defense sectors. My work spans the full embedded stack — bare-metal firmware, RTOS platforms, Qt/QML HMI, IIoT data pipelines, and cloud integration.

Currently I'm building smart energy meters (NexRev) and an AI-driven school security system (Lockout) using PSoC 4-BLE, Azure IoT, and GCP — while also contributing to GM's Ultifi SDV platform and Hyundai's IIoT Fleet Management (ESP32S3 + FreeRTOS + AWS IoT).

Early in my career, I developed missile launch applications for BRAHMOS on Lynx RTOS at ECIL/DRDL — experience that instilled a deep respect for safety-critical, zero-defect software engineering.

Beyond engineering, I publish technical articles on LinkedIn and InfoServices, and have built an AI-powered profile chatbot hosted on Hugging Face. Currently open to Senior Embedded / IoT Architect roles in the UAE and globally.
